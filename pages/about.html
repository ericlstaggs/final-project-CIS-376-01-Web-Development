In 1961 the Muscle Shoals Sailing Club was established on beautiful Lake Wilson by a group of men and women dedicated to the art of sailing.

As a nonprofit organization, we provide sailing through instruction, regattas, races, family sailing and fun events at our beautiful clubhouse.
