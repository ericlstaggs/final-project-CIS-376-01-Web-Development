Sailing Seminar

A two-day training seminar on June 6-7 with classroom lessons and hands-on coaching. Gain confidence in sail trim, docking, teamwork, and basic seamanship.

  • Two-day workshop
  • Classroom + water practice
  • Perfect for beginners

What to Expect

Experience the joy of sailing while you learn essential terminology, rigging, safety, and boat handling. Each session is paced for new sailors and designed to build confidence on the water.

Our one-day events give you a taste of sailing, while the seminar provides a complete foundation with classroom instruction and guided sea time.
