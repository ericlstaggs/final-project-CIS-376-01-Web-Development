Muscle Shoals Sailing Club is a non-profit corporation which owns the land, club house, docks and other facilities its members utilize.

Full membership in the club is termed a Class A Membership which conveys a part ownership of the Club's assets and voting privileges. To introduce the Club and sailing to juniors or students we have a membership which provides full access to the club facilities but does not convey ownership or voting rights. Land storage of boats, wet slips, and rack storage of Lasers is available for an additional fee.

  • Initiation Fee for Class A Membership $300
  • Annual Dues for Class A Membership $600
  • Annual Dues for Class B Membership $200 (available for ages 23-29)
  • Annual Dues for Junior Membership $25
  • Slip Storage: $28 per foot per year
  • Land storage: $150 per year
  • Laser rack: $80 per year
  • Trailer storage: $60 per year
